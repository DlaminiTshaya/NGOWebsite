About Us

Dikgatlong Green Initiative (DGI) – In 2018 LLB hosted 15 youth, this was the first time for us in the Department of Environment’s Youth Environmental Services (YES) Programme. In 2019 we have taken on 5 youth for the program. These youths are active in our organization to implement our projects, at the same time receiving mentorship and training. The DGI has been approved funding through the revised EPIP program 2018/19-2022/23 MTSF and as the owning entity LLB are focused on the below interventions;

  • Open Space Management and Greening
  • Establishment of a plant nursery
  • Wetland Management
  • Clean Up Campaigns
  • Climate Change Mitigation
  • Waste Management ( Focus on the 3 R’s)
  • Environmental Awareness and Education

    • Our Mission

    LLB focuses on proper management of terrestrial ecosystems to ensure the well-being and resilience of our communities, through biodiversity conservation, combating desertification, climate change mitigation and adaptation. As an organisation LLB believes youths should be actively involved in solution implementation, to this end we provide guidance and support to youth development programmes to mitigate poverty and empower present and future communities through:

  • Working with them to influence social, economic and environmental systems
  • Improving and building sustainable capabilities of the youth, predominantly women
  • Increase access to knowledge, skills development and educational opportunities
  • Promote participation in sustainable community development projects, driving socio- economic development efforts to address myriad of challenges facing the youth.

    • Our Vision

    A robust circular economy with reduced or no environmental harm, in which sustainable development thrives with the efficient management of natural resources, for the benefit of youth and the environment providing a better quality of life for communities.

    Our Approach

    The LLB approach is to engage with communities to understand the culture and environment in which they live or work. We engage and work with different stakeholders in our efforts towards the following universal goals:

  • Sustainable access to clean energy
  • Quality education
  • Environmental conservation and sustainability
  • Zero Hunger

    • Embedded across our programme work are the following;

  • Projects to equip people and our local ecosystems to adapt to a changing climate.
  • Responsible consumption: Reduce.Reuse.Recycle
  • Education and Awareness
  • Poverty alleviation: To empower youth through various essential skills initiatives

    • Our Values

    In addition to our values, embedded in our organisation name, the below are values we embody when dealing with all our clients:

  • Sustainability
  • Community Focus
  • Knowledge & skills sharing
  • Humanity
  • Love
